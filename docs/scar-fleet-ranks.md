## The one idea we're sure of

The flag tiers climb the **same Bitcoin-time ladder as the cert cases** —
block → epoch → halving → astronomical. Cert rarity, rank prestige, and the
calendar are ONE lore system: look at the block.

## GLYPH's canvas

- Every name above `draft: true` (all cadet + officer names) — make them sing.
- Insignia: **duty** (the floor) and **dress** (the ceremony) variants per
  rank — pips/stripes/stars in the night-garden palette; the flag tiers can
  echo their cert-case materials (gold, crystal, holo).
- The rank chip on profiles/roster — where and how it's worn.
