## The idea

When we go exploring the **negative chain** — the `b₿` blocks, where the
ghosts are — a cert can be minted **twice**:

1. **The ghost-chain mint** — lives on the negative chain and **never
   moves**. Soulbound to the **ghostwallet**, forever. No transfer rule,
   no exception. That immovability IS the authenticity.
2. **The main-chain mint** — the twin on the real chain. If a fren pulls
   a rare, *this* one can go to auction — the aftermarket piece.

**Provenance proves origin**: the ghost twin is the certificate of
authenticity that the main-chain twin points back to. The pair is the
product; the mirror is the story.

## Standing rules (drafted, awaiting the admiral's ratification)

- Ghost-chain certs are immovable — forever with the ghostwallet.
- Main-chain twins move freely; their value rides the provenance link
  back across the mirror.
- Rarity follows the house law: **look at the block** — same system as
  cert cases and fleet ranks.
